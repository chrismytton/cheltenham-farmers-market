Cheltenham farmers markets dates

Cheltenham council provide a list of farmers market dates on their website. I thought it would be useful to have it on my calendar so I remembered to go when it was on. The website doesn't provide anything you can download for your calendar, so I wrote a scraper to scrape the dates and convert them to iCalendar format.
